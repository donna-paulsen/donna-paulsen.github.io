🧠 The B2B Thesis

The B2C app creates a player data asset that no Colombian cancha platform has: who plays, how often, what skill level, when they prefer to play, and who they play with. The B2B product monetizes this data by giving court owners tools they've never had — without touching a single payment.

The Court Owner's Reality

The average Colombian cancha sintética owner:

  • Spends 2-3 hours/day on WhatsApp answering the same questions: "¿Hay cancha mañana a las 7?" "¿Cuánto cuesta la hora?" "¿Tienen parqueadero?"
  • Loses 20-30% of bookings to no-shows with no deposit system and no penalty mechanism
  • Has zero customer data. Regulars are "el man del jueves" in his head. When they stop coming, he has no way to know or reach out
  • Dead hours (10am-4pm weekdays) hemorrhage money. Fixed costs don't pause. He has no tools to fill those slots beyond an Instagram post nobody sees
  • Misses leads at night. Someone messages at 11pm about Saturday. He's asleep. By morning, they've called another court
The core pitch: "Your court has an AI assistant that answers WhatsApp 24/7, knows your schedule, your prices, and your customers. It never sleeps, never forgets, and costs less than one day's booking revenue per month."

🤖 Feature 1: AI Agents (The Killer Differentiator)

📅
The Scheduler Agent
Answers availability questions via WhatsApp 24/7. Never sleeps, never double-books.
AI Pro Plan

How it works: Connected to the court's real-time booking calendar. When a lead messages on WhatsApp asking about availability, the agent checks the calendar, responds with open slots, and can tentatively hold one pending confirmation.

Example Conversation:

👤 Lead (11:47pm)
Buenas noches, hay cancha disponible para el sábado a las 3pm?
🤖 CanchaScore AI (11:47pm)
¡Hola! 👋 Déjame revisar... El sábado a las 3pm está ocupada, pero tenemos disponible a las 2pm y a las 4pm. ¿Te sirve alguna de esas? La hora está a $150.000 COP en horario de fin de semana.
👤 Lead
A las 4pm perfecto, me la reservan?
🤖 CanchaScore AI
¡Listo! Te reservo el sábado a las 4pm. La cancha queda reservada por 2 horas. Te llega un recordatorio el viernes. ¿A nombre de quién la pongo? 😊
  • Response time: Under 5 seconds, 24/7. Vs. court owner who replies in 2-12 hours (or never)
  • Accuracy: Connected to real-time calendar — never says "yes" when there's no slot
  • Handoff: For complex requests it can't handle, escalates to the owner with context: "This person wants to book a 3-hour block with BBQ service — needs your attention"
  • Language: Natural Colombian Spanish. No robotic responses. Trained per-court with their specific pricing, services, and personality
💬
The Receptionist Agent
Handles general inquiries — prices, hours, services, directions. The FAQ that never gets tired.
AI Pro Plan

How it works: Trained on each court's specific information — pricing tiers (peak/off-peak/weekend), services (cafetería, parking, lockers, uniforms for rent), location details, capacity, and policies. Answers any question a walk-in would ask the front desk.

  • Knows price differences: weekday vs weekend, morning vs night, league vs casual
  • Shares Google Maps location link on request
  • Handles multiple conversations simultaneously — something the owner physically can't do
  • Learns from conversations: if customers keep asking about a service you don't advertise, you'll see it in analytics
📣
The Re-engagement Agent
Proactively reaches out to lapsed players. Fills dead hours. Turns churned customers into returning ones.
AI Premium Plan

How it works: Uses CRM data from the B2C app + booking history to identify players who haven't visited in 2-4 weeks. Sends personalized WhatsApp messages with targeted offers for dead hours.

🤖 CanchaScore AI (Tuesday 10:00am)
¡Ey Juan! 👋 Hace 3 semanas que no vienes a jugar. Te extrañamos en la cancha. Este jueves tenemos la hora de 2pm a 4pm con 30% de descuento. ¿Le metemos? 🔥
  • Targets dead hours specifically: Only offers discounts for low-occupancy slots, protecting peak revenue
  • Personalized: Uses the player's name, last visit date, and preferred time slots. Not a mass blast
  • Rate-limited: Maximum 1 outreach per player per 2 weeks. Not spam — targeted re-engagement
  • Measurable: Court owner sees "Re-engagement campaign: 45 messages sent, 12 opened, 4 bookings recovered"
The math: If this agent recovers just 4 dead-hour bookings per month at $30/hour average, that's $120/month recovered — more than the cost of the entire Premium plan. ROI is immediate.

Real-time calendar that syncs with AI agents, the player app, and the dashboard.
Starter Plan

👥 Feature 3: Customer CRM

📊
Player Intelligence CRM
Every player who books or plays at your court becomes a rich data profile — not just a WhatsApp name.
Starter Plan

This is where the B2C app data becomes incredibly valuable for court owners:

  • Player profile: Name, phone, preferred days/times, average frequency, total spend, skill level, crew affiliations
  • Segmentation: Group customers by frequency (weekly regulars, monthly casuals, churned), time preference (morning, evening, weekend), and skill level
  • Churn detection: Automatic flag when a regular hasn't booked in 3+ weeks. "⚠️ La Piquera del Parque hasn't booked in 4 weeks — they used to come every Thursday"
  • Lifetime value tracking: See how much each player/crew has spent over time. Identify and protect your most valuable customers
  • Notes: Court staff can add notes to any player: "Prefers court 2, always asks for cold water, organizer for a 15-person group"
The data advantage: Because players use the CanchaScore B2C app independently, the court gets profile data they never asked for — skill ratings, match frequency, crew size, social connections. This is data that iCancha and traditional booking tools simply don't have.

🔄 How the B2C App Powers the B2B Product

This is the flywheel that makes CanchaScore's B2B fundamentally different from competitors like iCancha:

👤→🏟
Players → Courts

Players use the B2C app to log matches and track their skills. When they book a court through the app or check in, the court automatically gets a rich customer profile — skill level, play frequency, crew size, preferred times — data that would take years to build manually.

🏟→👤
Courts → Players

Courts use AI agents and smart promotions to reach players with relevant offers. Players get better experiences (less WhatsApp chaos, faster booking, balanced games). The court's tools make the player's life better, which drives more B2C engagement.

Data That Only CanchaScore Has:

  • Player skill ratings — allows courts to organize skill-tiered leagues and tournaments
  • Crew composition — knows who plays with whom, enabling "invite the whole crew" marketing
  • Cross-court behavior — knows when a regular starts playing at a competitor's court (churn signal)
  • Social graph — when one player in a crew discovers a new court, the re-engagement agent can invite the whole crew
  • Match frequency trends — predicts seasonal demand patterns and suggests pricing adjustments

🤝 Sales Strategy — How to Sell to Cancheros

The Demo That Sells Itself

You walk into the court. You ask the owner: "Can I see the last 5 WhatsApp messages from customers?" He shows you. They're all the same: "¿Hay cancha?" "¿Cuánto cuesta?" "¿A qué hora abren?"

You say: "What if I showed you an AI that answers all of those, right now, with your real schedule and your real prices?"

You pull out your phone. You demo the AI answering his real questions in real time on WhatsApp. He watches his own FAQs being answered perfectly in 3 seconds.

That's the close. No deck. No pitch. Just the demo.

Onboarding Flow

  • Day 1 (15 min): Collect court info — name, address, courts, pricing, hours, services. Upload to the AI
  • Day 2: Set up booking calendar with existing schedule
  • Day 3: Connect WhatsApp Business API to court's number
  • Day 4: AI agents go live. 2-week free trial begins
  • Day 14: Show results: "Your AI handled 47 conversations. 12 led to bookings. 3 were at 11pm when you were asleep." Convert to paid
Honest limitation: Onboarding requires in-person visits. You can't sell to court owners via email or ads. The first 50 courts will each require a physical visit. Budget 2-3 hours per court for demo + onboarding. After 50 courts, word-of-mouth among court owners kicks in.
